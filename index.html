About

Hello, and thanks for taking the time to read my page. I started teaching myself programming at the start of 2017 as a New Year’s Resolution to be more productive with my free time. At first it was just a hobby, but after some serious thought I realized I wanted to make a living off of it. I have knowledge of both front-end and back-end technologies, although recently, I have really enjoyed using React JS for the front of the stack. React has made state management in my projects easier, and has allowed me to better visualize the build process for a web app compared to vanilla javascript.

Here’s a fun fact: In the near future, I want to release my own 40 plus hour course on web development. I have a passion for teaching, and I have noticed that there are not many online courses out there that teach full stack web development from scratch. The few full stack courses that do exist are either outdated or felt rushed and of low quality.

If I am not coding or watching a concert, you will likely find me eating at a Chic-Fil-A. I also plan on creating a blog site using the MERN stack, and learning Python on the side with whatever free time I have. I just graduated from San Diego State University with a Bachelor of Arts in Public Administration.
